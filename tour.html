Rama krishna beach

Rama Krishna Beach in Vizag is a popular coastal destination known for its picturesque shoreline and vibrant atmosphere. Visitors can enjoy stunning sunsets, leisurely strolls along the sandy beach, and the local street food. The beach also features attractions like the beautifully sculpted Visakha Museum and a lively promenade.

Rushikonda beach

Rushikonda Beach is a serene escape with golden sands and clear waters, perfect for swimming and sunbathing. Its scenic beauty and tranquil environment make it a favorite for relaxing and enjoying water sports. The beach is also known for its picturesque sunsets and clean surroundings.

Indira gandhi zoo park

Ross hill church

Ross Hill Church, perched on a scenic hilltop, offers stunning panoramic views of Vizag. This historic church, known for its serene ambiance and beautiful architecture, is a peaceful place for reflection and prayer. The site is also renowned for its charming colonial-era design and tranquil surroundings.
